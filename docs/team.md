## DevOps Culture
- Break down silos & shared ownership
- Automation-first mindset & self-service
- Continuous improvement & blameless postmortems

- Define Deployment Frequency, Lead Time, MTTR, Change Failure Rate
- Establish SLOs & SLIs for critical services
- Progress through maturity levels (Manual → Automated → Integrated → Optimized)
